**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026
**Status:** Draft

## Executive Summary

OpenSims.ai is a human-in-the-loop AI simulation platform that enables users to participate in diverse simulated environments as actors, guiding AI-driven worlds toward defined objectives. By combining AI automation with human decision-making, OpenSims.ai creates a gamified ecosystem where users earn OSCoins (redeemable for real-world cash) by completing objectives, making strategic decisions, and preventing AI systems from deviating from their goals.

---

## Vision & Mission

### Vision
To become the world's leading platform for human-AI collaborative simulations, where anyone can create, explore, and profit from infinite simulated realities.

### Mission
Empower individuals to shape AI-driven simulations through meaningful human intervention while building a sustainable economy that rewards quality decision-making and creative problem-solving.

---

## Problem Statement

1. **AI Hallucination & Deviation**: Autonomous AI systems often deviate from objectives or generate unrealistic outcomes without human oversight
2. **Lack of Engaging HITL Platforms**: Current human-in-the-loop systems are primarily enterprise-focused and lack gamification
3. **Limited Monetization for Micro-Tasks**: Existing platforms don't adequately reward users for high-quality decision-making
4. **Barrier to AI Experimentation**: Creating and testing AI simulations requires technical expertise, limiting accessibility

## Product Overview

### Core Concept
OpenSims.ai is a platform where:
- **Environments** are simulated worlds with specific objectives (e.g., "Build a sustainable city," "Run a successful startup," "Manage a crisis response")
- **Actors** are roles users can assume within environments (e.g., Mayor, CEO, Doctor, Engineer)
- **Tools/Software** are AI-powered capabilities actors use to make decisions and take actions
- **Human-in-the-Loop** intervention guides the AI, making critical decisions and course corrections
- **OSCoins** reward successful objective completion and quality decision-making

## Key Features

### 1. Simulation Environment System

#### 1.1 Environment Structure
Each environment contains:
- **Objective**: Clear success criteria (e.g., "Achieve 90% customer satisfaction in 6 months")
- **Actors**: 1-10 roles with defined responsibilities
- **Tools**: AI-powered software actors can use (analytics, communication, resource management)
- **Time Scale**: Real-time, accelerated (1 hour = 1 day), or turn-based
- **Complexity Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert

#### 1.2 Pre-Built Environments (Launch Set)
1. **Startup Founder** - Build and scale a tech company
2. **City Planner** - Design and manage a sustainable city
3. **Crisis Manager** - Handle emergency response scenarios
4. **Research Director** - Lead scientific research projects
5. **Diplomatic Envoy** - Navigate international relations
6. **Hospital Administrator** - Optimize healthcare operations
7. **Mars Colony Leader** - Establish thriving off-world settlement
8. **Medieval Kingdom Ruler** - Manage resources, military, and politics

#### 1.3 User-Generated Environments
- **Creation Interface**: No-code builder with guided templates
- **Parameter System**: Creators define objectives, actors, tools, and rules within platform constraints
- **Validation System**: AI checks environment logic and feasibility
- **Publishing**: Environments go through review before public listing
- **Monetization**: Creators earn 20% of OSCoins spent by players in their environments

### 2. Actor System

#### 2.1 Actor Selection
- Browse available actors across active environments
- View actor difficulty, time commitment, and potential OSCoin rewards
- See how many humans are currently active vs. AI-controlled
- Matchmaking system pairs users with suitable environments based on skill level

#### 2.2 Actor Capabilities
- **Decision Points**: AI presents choices at critical moments requiring human judgment
- **Override Authority**: Humans can override AI decisions and provide alternative strategies
- **Tool Access**: Use AI-powered tools specific to the role (financial models, resource planners, communication systems)
- **Collaboration**: Multiple human actors can work together in the same environment

### 3. Human-in-the-Loop Mechanics

#### 3.1 Decision Points
- **Critical Decisions**: High-impact choices requiring human judgment (strategic pivots, ethical dilemmas)
- **Quality Control**: Review AI-generated outputs (documents, plans, designs)
- **Anomaly Detection**: Identify when AI is deviating from objectives
- **Creative Input**: Provide ideas the AI can't generate independently

#### 3.2 Intervention Triggers
- **Scheduled Check-ins**: Periodic reviews at defined intervals
- **Threshold Alerts**: AI requests help when confidence drops below threshold
- **Deviation Alerts**: System detects drift from objectives
- **Time-Sensitive**: Urgent decisions requiring immediate human input

#### 3.3 Decision Quality Scoring
- **Outcome-Based**: Long-term impact on objective completion
- **Efficiency**: Speed and resource optimization
- **Creativity**: Novel solutions vs. standard approaches
- **Alignment**: How well decisions match defined objectives

### 4. OSCoin Economy

#### 4.1 Earning OSCoins
- **Objective Completion**: 50-5,000 OSCoins based on difficulty
- **One-Time Quests**: 100-10,000 OSCoins for unique achievements per environment
- **Decision Quality Bonuses**: 10-500% multipliers for exceptional decisions
- **Daily Streaks**: Bonus multipliers for consistent participation
- **Referral Rewards**: 10% of referred users' first-year earnings

#### 4.2 Quest System
Each environment has unique quests:
- **Hidden Objectives**: Discover and complete undocumented goals
- **Speed Runs**: Complete objectives in record time
- **Perfect Scores**: Achieve 100% success on all metrics
- **Creative Solutions**: Find novel approaches to standard problems
- **Community Challenges**: Compete with other users on leaderboards

#### 4.3 OSCoin Redemption
- **Minimum Withdrawal**: 10,000 OSCoins ($10 USD)
- **Exchange Rate**: Dynamic, starting at 1,000 OSCoins = $1 USD
- **Payout Methods**: PayPal, Stripe, cryptocurrency, bank transfer
- **Processing Time**: 3-5 business days
- **Verification**: KYC required for withdrawals over $100

#### 4.4 Economic Sustainability
- **Revenue Sources**:
  - Premium environments (20% platform fee)
  - Enterprise subscriptions
  - Data licensing (anonymized simulation outcomes)
  - Creator revenue share (80% to creators, 20% to platform)
- **Payout Cap**: Maximum 70% of monthly revenue allocated to OSCoin redemptions
- **Reserve Fund**: 15% of revenue held for economic stability

### 5. Real-World Data Integration

#### 5.1 Data Types
- **Market Data**: Financial metrics, consumer trends
- **Professional Expertise**: Domain-specific knowledge from verified experts
- **Current Events**: Recent news affecting simulation scenarios
- **User-Submitted Data**: Photos, documents, measurements (with privacy controls)

#### 5.2 Data Contribution System
- **Opt-In Only**: Users choose which simulations can request data
- **Data Types Clearly Labeled**: Specify exactly what data is needed and why
- **Privacy Protection**: All PII is anonymized before use
- **Compensation**: Bonus OSCoins for valuable data contributions (50-500 per submission)
- **Verification**: Data is validated before integration into simulations

#### 5.3 Use Cases
- **Startup Simulation**: Real market research data improves product-market fit analysis
- **City Planning**: Current infrastructure data and demographic information
- **Crisis Management**: Real-time news and situation reports
- **Medical Scenarios**: Anonymized case studies and treatment outcomes

### 6. Platform Capabilities

#### 6.1 Environment Creation Tools
- **Template Library**: 20+ starting templates
- **Visual Builder**: Drag-and-drop interface for defining actors and objectives
- **Rules Engine**: Define constraints, triggers, and success criteria
- **AI Tool Configuration**: Select from library of pre-built AI capabilities
- **Testing Sandbox**: Simulate environment before publishing
- **Versioning**: Update environments while preserving active simulations

#### 6.2 Simulation Engine
- **Multi-Agent AI System**: Each actor (human or AI) operates independently
- **Real-Time Processing**: Handle 1,000+ concurrent simulations
- **State Management**: Persistent world state across sessions
- **Branching Scenarios**: Environments adapt based on human decisions
- **Rollback Capability**: Undo critical mistakes (limited uses)

#### 6.3 Social Features
- **Leaderboards**: Top performers per environment and overall
- **Replay System**: Watch successful simulation runs
- **Community Forums**: Discuss strategies and share tips
- **Team Simulations**: Invite friends to join as co-actors
- **Streaming Integration**: Share gameplay on Twitch/YouTube

#### 6.4 Learning & Progression
- **Tutorial Environments**: Guided introductions to platform mechanics
- **Skill Trees**: Unlock advanced environments and tools
- **Achievement Badges**: Visual recognition for milestones
- **Performance Analytics**: Personal dashboard showing strengths and improvement areas
- **Mentor System**: Experienced users can guide newcomers (earn bonus OSCoins)

## User Journey

### New User Onboarding
1. **Sign-Up**: Email/Google/GitHub authentication
2. **Welcome Survey**: Interests, experience level, time availability
3. **Tutorial**: Complete 5-minute guided simulation (earn first 100 OSCoins)
4. **First Simulation**: Matched with beginner-friendly environment
5. **First Decision**: Make first human-in-the-loop decision
6. **Quest Unlock**: Discover quest system
7. **Social Connection**: Option to join community Discord/forums

### Regular User Flow
1. **Browse Environments**: Filter by difficulty, time, rewards
2. **Select Actor**: Choose role within chosen environment
3. **Enter Simulation**: AI briefs on current situation
4. **Make Decisions**: Respond to decision points as they arise
5. **Complete Objectives**: Work toward environment goals
6. **Earn OSCoins**: Receive rewards for performance
7. **Complete Quests**: Pursue one-time achievements
8. **Withdraw Earnings**: Redeem OSCoins for real money

### Creator Journey
1. **Access Creator Portal**: Unlock after completing 3 simulations
2. **Choose Template**: Start with pre-built framework
3. **Customize Environment**: Define actors, objectives, tools
4. **Test Simulation**: Run internal tests with AI actors
5. **Submit for Review**: Platform validation (24-48 hours)
6. **Publish**: Environment goes live
7. **Monitor Performance**: Analytics on usage and user feedback
8. **Earn Revenue**: Receive creator share of OSCoins

## Technical Requirements

### Frontend
- **Framework**: React 18+ or Vue 3
- **Hosting**: GitHub Pages compatible (static site generation)
- **UI Library**: Tailwind CSS for responsive design
- **State Management**: Redux or Zustand
- **Real-Time Updates**: WebSocket connection for live simulation updates

### Backend (Future Phase - MVP Uses Client-Side Only)
- **API**: REST + GraphQL for complex queries
- **Real-Time**: WebSocket server for simulation updates
- **Database**: PostgreSQL for user data, MongoDB for simulation states
- **AI Integration**: OpenAI API, Anthropic Claude API, or custom models
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe Connect for OSCoin redemptions

### MVP Scope (GitHub Pages Deployment)
- **Static Demo**: Pre-scripted simulations with branching scenarios
- **Client-Side Logic**: JavaScript-based decision tree
- **Mock AI**: Predetermined AI responses that feel dynamic
- **Simulated Economy**: Track OSCoins locally (localStorage)
- **Limited Environments**: 3 pre-built simulations

### Security & Compliance
- **Authentication**: OAuth 2.0, JWT tokens
- **Data Encryption**: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **GDPR Compliance**: Data export, deletion rights
- **KYC/AML**: Identity verification for payouts over $100
- **Content Moderation**: Review user-generated environments for ToS compliance

### Performance Requirements
- **Page Load**: <2 seconds on 3G connection
- **Decision Latency**: <500ms from user input to AI response
- **Concurrent Users**: 10,000+ simultaneous active simulations
- **Uptime**: 99.9% SLA

## Risk Analysis

### Technical Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| AI hallucination produces harmful content | High | Content filtering, human moderation, community reporting |
| Scalability issues with concurrent simulations | High | Load testing, auto-scaling infrastructure, queue system |
| Data privacy breach | Critical | Security audits, encryption, compliance certifications |
| WebSocket connection instability | Medium | Fallback to polling, connection retry logic, state persistence |

### Business Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
| OSCoin economy inflation | High | Dynamic exchange rates, revenue caps, reserve fund |
| Low user retention | High | Engaging content pipeline, social features, improved onboarding |
| Creator exploitation concerns | Medium | Clear ToS, fair revenue split, creator support program |
| Payment processor limitations | Medium | Multiple payment partners, cryptocurrency options |

### Legal Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Real-money gaming regulations | Critical | Legal counsel, jurisdiction-specific compliance, licensing |
| User-generated content liability | High | Content moderation, DMCA process, clear community guidelines |
| Data collection restrictions (GDPR, CCPA) | High | Privacy-first design, consent management, regional compliance |
| Financial regulations (money transmitter laws) | Critical | Partner with licensed payment processors, legal structure |

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors
1. **AI Dungeon / Latitude**
   - Strength: Established AI storytelling platform
   - Weakness: No monetization for players, less structured objectives
   - Differentiation: OpenSims offers real cash rewards and structured goals

2. **Simulation Games (SimCity, Civilization)**
   - Strength: Engaging simulation mechanics
   - Weakness: Fully AI-controlled, no real-world economic benefits
   - Differentiation: Human-in-the-loop collaboration with AI

3. **Microwork Platforms (MTurk, Scale AI)**
   - Strength: Established payout systems
   - Weakness: Repetitive tasks, no gamification
   - Differentiation: Engaging gameplay with strategic decision-making

### Indirect Competitors
- **Play-to-Earn Games (Axie Infinity)**: Blockchain-based earning, but crypto-volatile
- **GPT-based Simulation Tools**: Developer-focused, not consumer-friendly
- **Traditional Gaming Platforms**: No real money earning (except esports)

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Unique Value Proposition**: Only platform combining AI simulations + human-in-the-loop + real cash rewards
2. **Creator Economy**: Democratizes simulation creation without coding
3. **Ethical AI**: Human oversight prevents harmful AI outputs
4. **Accessible Earning**: Lower barrier than esports, more engaging than microwork

## Monetization Strategy

### Revenue Streams
1. **Premium Environments (Freemium Model)**
   - Free: 60% of environments
   - Premium: 40% of environments ($2-10 entry fee in OSCoins)

2. **Creator Revenue Share**
   - Platform takes 20% of OSCoins spent in user-generated environments

### Glossary
- **Environment**: A simulated world with defined objectives
- **Actor**: A role within an environment that users can assume
- **HITL (Human-in-the-Loop)**: System requiring human input at critical decision points
- **OSCoin**: Platform currency redeemable for real-world money
- **Quest**: One-time achievement within an environment for bonus rewards
- **Simulation**: Active instance of an environment being played
- **Decision Point**: Moment when AI requires human judgment
